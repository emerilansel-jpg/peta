✅ Cair mulai Rp150K

Jangan sampai keduluan. Klaim spot kamu sekarang sebelum batch ini full:

## Version B — Easy Money ("Gampang Dapet Cuan")

Angle: low effort + fast payoff. Lean on "kerja santai dari hape, langsung dapet Rp50K".

```
Halo, kabar baik! 💸

PeTa udah live — cara paling santai buat dapet duit tambahan dari hape. Gak perlu skill, gak perlu modal, gak perlu jualan apa-apa.

Begitu daftar, kamu langsung dapet:
💰 Bonus Rp50.000 cuma dari ngelarin 5 langkah onboarding (~10 menit)
💰 Tiap task selanjutnya dibayar Rp5.000–Rp20.000
💰 Withdraw mulai Rp150.000, transfer ke rekening kamu

Cocok buat ngisi waktu rebahan jadi cuan. Anak kuliah, ibu rumah tangga, karyawan side-hustle — semua bisa.

Daftar sekarang, gratis:

👉 https://penghasilantambahan.com
```

Char count: ~597.

---

## Recommendation

**Send Version B first.** The waitlist already self-selected for interest, so FOMO has weaker bite — they're not wavering, they're waiting for the on-ramp. Version B removes friction and quantifies the payoff in the first 30 seconds ("Rp50K in 10 minutes"), which converts warm leads better than scarcity pressure they may not believe yet. Hold Version A as the day-3 follow-up to anyone who didn't click.
