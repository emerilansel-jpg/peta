## Quick Synthesis (untuk PeTa)

- **Gap #1 — Anti-fraud:** Microworkers' success-rate gating is battle-tested. PeTa's `min_level` + admin approval is a start, but no automated reputation decay yet.
- **Gap #2 — Payout UX:** Sribu + Toluna pay via OVO/GoPay/Dana. PeTa min Rp150K bank transfer is the slowest option in this list.
- **Gap #3 — Referral depth:** YSense's multi-tier referral creates compounding growth. PeTa's flat Rp20K both-sides is simpler but caps virality.
- **PeTa's edge:** Reddit-specialized + Bahasa-first onboarding + curated task quality. Defensible if executed well.

## Confidence / Caveats

- Pricing numbers are public-facing ranges from memory + 2024-era data. Margins, payout minimums, and feature sets shift quarterly — verify before quoting externally.
- Picoworkers rebranded to Rapidworkers around 2023; same operator. Listed as the SEA-relevant entity.
- Sribu/Projects.co.id are not direct PeTa competitors (project-based, not micro-task) but compete for the same Indonesian "earn online" mindshare — included for that reason.
- Toluna/YouGov are survey-only, not comment/upvote — included because their **payout rail** is the relevant benchmark.
